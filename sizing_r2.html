Technical Explanations

  • Power & SFC: Fuel weight is calculated using the input volume. Range is calculated based on the Engine HP and the selected SFC efficiency.
  • Stall & Landing: Selecting "Full Flaps" increases the maximum lift coefficient (CLmax) to 1.90, which reduces stall speed and ground roll.
  • Static Margin: A measure of longitudinal stability; 10-15% is ideal for a stable, easy-to-fly aircraft.
