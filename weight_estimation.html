Warehouse Steel Estimator

Results (St 37 Steel):
Technical Assumptions
  • Material: Structural Steel Grade St 37 (S235 JR).
  • Bay Spacing: Calculated based on 6-meter intervals.
  • Height: Eave height of 4 to 6 meters.
  • Structural System: Built-up tapered/I-sections for main frames; Cold-formed Z/C purlins for secondary steel.
  • Allowances: Includes 15% for connections (plates, bolts, stiffeners) and ~3-4 kg/m² for roof/wall bracing.
Limitations
  • No Cranes: Calculation assumes no overhead crane loads or heavy mezzanine levels.
  • Standard Wind: Assumes non-cyclonic/standard wind zones.
  • Soil Conditions: Does not include weight of steel reinforcement for foundations.
  • Seismic: High seismic zones may require additional bracing weight not covered here.
  • Cladding: Weight of sandwich panels or sheets is NOT included in the steel tonnage.
