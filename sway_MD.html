Moment Distribution Method: Sway Frame

1. Non-Sway Analysis
  • Calculate FEM for all members based on gravity loads.
  • Perform MDM (Distribution & Carry-Over) until joints balance.
  • Calculate column shears: V = (M_top + M_bot) / L.
  • Sum shears to find restraining force R.
2. Sway Analysis
  • Apply arbitrary displacement Δ.
  • Assume initial sway moments (e.g., 100 kNm) proportional to I/L².
  • Distribute moments until balanced.
  • Sum resulting shears to find sway force S.
3. Final Superposition
Correction Factor: k = R / S
M_final = M_non-sway + (k * M_sway)
